About Department of Biomedical Engineering

Biomedical Engineering is a multidisciplinary field that blends biology and engineering. This discipline encompasses the profound knowledge of biological principles in the realm of engineering design. The department of Biomedical Engineering was established in the year 2009 and has maintained an exceptional track record in academics, placement, industrial training and research activities. Biomedical engineering has a direct effect on human life thus making it different and attractive from other streams of engineering. The ever-increasing demand for Biomedical Engineers is pertinent to our societyâ€™s affinity towards efficient and convenient healthcare. Biomedical engineering being a interdisciplinary field has almost endless possibilities and there is growing need of large scale investments to endorse quality education to train biomedical engineers to provide innovative and cost effective health care solutions.

About the conference

This conference aims to bring together eminent scientists, scholars, research fellows, students, biomedical industry professionals, members of associations, policy makers and academic professionals from different countries across the globe to exchange and share their experiences and research results on all aspects of Biomedical Engineering. The conference aims to empower future generations in different fields of Biomedical Engineering to explore exciting research outcomes that lie in tune with the objectives and theme of this exciting event. It further helps you enhance your understanding of commercial, scientific and regulatory issues that affect strategic decision making in the biomedical sector.

Each track will witness technical talks by eminent personalities from across the nation to ignite creative thinking and kindle research solutions.

All submitted abstracts/ papers will go through a blind peer review process and accepted manuscripts will be published in conference proceedings.

Selected conference papers will be published in special / Scopus indexed journals associated with this conference. Associated journals will allocate a special / regular issue for papers submitted to this conference.
