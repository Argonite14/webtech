ICNADBE - 2023

1st International Conference on Novel Approaches and Developments in Biomedical Engineering Organized by: Department of Biomedical Engineering Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences, Coimbatore

ICNADBE - 2023

24th - 25th August 2023

Biomedical Engineering is a multidisciplinary field that blends biology and engineering. This discipline encompasses the profound knowledge of biological principles in the realm of engineering design. The department of Biomedical Engineering was established in the year 2009 and has maintained an exceptional track record in academics, placement, industrial training and research activities. Biomedical engineering has a direct effect on human life thus making it different and attractive from other streams of engineering. The ever-increasing demand for Biomedical Engineers is pertinent to our society’s affinity towards efficient and convenient healthcare. Biomedical engineering being a interdisciplinary field has almost endless possibilities and there is growing need of large scale investments to endorse quality education to train biomedical engineers to provide innovative and cost effective health care solutions.
